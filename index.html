Our Mission
At Lovely Professional University, we are committed to fostering an environment where biodiversity thrives. Our plant database is part of our initiative to educate students and visitors about the incredible variety of plant life in our campus. At the heart of our mission is a deep love for nature and a commitment to sharing knowledge. Our plant database, enriched with QR codes for easy access, is the result of a collaborative effort between two dedicated individuals with distinct but complementary roles.
Key Features
  • QR Code Integration :
    Each plant has it's own QR. Just select the plant name and scan the QR to get the information.
  • Detailed Plant Profiles :
    Our database provides common name, scientific name, origin, family, kingdom and order of the plants.
  • Explore with Ease :
    Explore the greenary of LPU campus with ease using our database.
